--> Most of the modern machine can perform 10 ^ 8 operation /second
	
	Constraint	|	Time Complexity	
	-------------------------------------
	<= [10...11]	|	O(n!)
	<  [15...18]	|	O(2^n * n^2)
	<  100		|	O(n^4)
	<  400		|	O(n^3)
	<  2000		|	O(n^2 * log(n))
	<  10^4		|	O(n^2)
	<  10^6		|	O(nLogn)
	<  10^8		|	O(n), O(log(n))
